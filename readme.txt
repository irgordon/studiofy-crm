A professional Photography Studio CRM. Manage clients, sync Google Calendar, send Square invoices, and collect digital signatures.

== Description ==

Studiofy CRM is a lightweight, high-performance Customer Relationship Management tool built specifically for photographers and creative studios.

**Core Features**
* **Client Management:** Convert leads from Contact Form 7 directly into CRM clients.
* **Google Calendar Integration:** Automatically push your studio bookings to your Google Calendar.
* **Square Payments:** Generate and email professional invoices directly via the Square API.
* **Digital Contracts:** Create legal agreements and capture digital signatures with audit trails (IP/Timestamp).
* **Performance Dashboard:** High-performance dashboard widget using transients.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `studiofy-crm` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to **Studiofy CRM > Settings** to configure your API Integrations.
